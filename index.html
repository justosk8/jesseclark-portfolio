About Me

Hello, I am Jesse Clark. I am currently a Territory Manager for MTD Products. I have been selling lawn mowers and snowblowers all over the Western United States for 12 years. I started in the landscaping industry in 1998 as a gardener at the University of Utah. I worked there in the summer and at Solitude Ski Resort in the winter for a couple years. I then got on full time at the University and started an apprenticeship to become a Master Gardener. After 8 years at the University, I got into the retail side of gardening and have been working at MTD ever since.

In my free time I am usually in the beautiful mountains surrounding Salt Lake City, Utah. In the warmer months you will find me riding my bike all over the Western States, or backpacking and fishing the Uinta Mountains. I really enjoy learning new things, and I am very excited to see where this coding journey takes me!
